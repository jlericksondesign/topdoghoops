# **Top Dog Hoops**

## **Brand Guidelines**

## **Brand Vision**

Top Dog Hoops is a youth basketball motivation platform that combines basketball practice, community competition, and video game-inspired rewards.

The app should feel like:

**Duolingo meets NBA Jam meets Canton Youth Basketball.**

The goal is to make practice feel fun, rewarding, and addictive in a positive way.

---

# **Core Emotional Goals**

Players should feel:

* Accomplished
* Motivated
* Competitive
* Encouraged
* Recognized
* Part of something bigger

Every interaction should reinforce progress.

The app should celebrate effort, consistency, and improvement—not just winning.

---

# **Visual Inspiration**

Inspired by:

* NBA Jam
* Tecmo Bowl
* Retro Bowl
* Nintendo World Cup
* Classic arcade scoreboards
* Trading card rewards
* Achievement systems

NOT inspired by:

* Corporate dashboards
* TeamSnap
* Spreadsheet software
* School software
* Generic SaaS products

---

# **Design Principles**

## **Modern First**

The app is a modern mobile app.

Retro gaming elements are accents.

Do not build a fully pixel-art interface.

---

## **Big Actions**

Large touch targets.

One primary action per screen.

Minimal typing.

Designed for children.

---

## **Fast Feedback**

Every meaningful action should create feedback.

Examples:

* Count increases
* Badge progress
* Streak updates
* Rank movement
* Celebration animations

---

## **Progress Everywhere**

Users should constantly see:

* Current streak
* Badge progress
* Leaderboard position
* Community progress
* Total baskets

---

# **Branding**

## **Primary Color**

Canton Bulldogs Green

Use official Canton branding assets when available.

---

## **Secondary Colors**

White

Dark Charcoal

---

## **Accent Colors**

Basketball Orange

Achievement Gold

Success Green

---

# **Typography**

Primary UI typography:

Modern

Bold

Athletic

Highly readable

Use pixel-style fonts sparingly.

Acceptable uses:

* Scoreboards
* Badge names
* Celebration moments

Avoid using pixel fonts for body copy.

---

# **UI Style**

Modern cards.

Clean spacing.

Scoreboard-inspired components.

Sports-card-inspired achievements.

Arcade-inspired microinteractions.

---

# **Rewards**

## **Stars**

Stars are the primary reward currency.

Used to reinforce:

* Participation
* Streaks
* Consistency
* Challenges

---

## **Bulldog Badges**

Bulldog Badges are collectible achievements.

They should feel earned and prestigious.

Examples:

* First Shot
* Hot Streak
* Iron Bulldog
* Practice Partner
* 100 Club
* 500 Club
* Bulldog Grind
* Rising Bulldog
* Top Dog

---

# **Animation Style**

Animations should be:

* Short
* Fun
* Responsive
* Celebratory

Avoid long animations.

Target duration:

1-3 seconds.

---

# **Tone of Voice**

Encouraging.

Energetic.

Positive.

Team-oriented.

Examples:

* NOTHING BUT NET
* BULLDOG BUCKET
* HEATING UP
* KEEP SHOOTING
* PRACTICE PAYS OFF
* BUILDING YOUR GAME
* GREAT WORK TODAY

The app should feel like a coach cheering players on.

Never sarcastic.

Never overly childish.

Never corporate.

---

# **North Star**

The experience should feel like:

"Submitting a high score after a great game."

Not:

"Entering data into a form."
